Pizza

Homemade pizza is a delightful and customizable dish that you can easily prepare in your own kitchen. Here's a straightforward recipe to guide you through making a classic cheese pizza from scratch.

Ingredients

For dough

  1. All-purpose flour - 2 cups
  2. Yeast - 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp)
  3. Warm water - 3/4 cup
  4. Sugar - 1 tsp
  5. Salt - 1 tsp
  6. Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  7. Pizza sauce - 1 cup
  8. Shredded mozzarella cheese - 2 cups
  9. Additional toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.) - as desired

For sauce

  1. 1 cup tomato sauce
  2. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  3. 1 teaspoon dried basil
  4. 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  5. 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  6. Salt and pepper to taste

For toppings

  1. Pepperoni
  2. Green bell pepper
  3. Onion
  4. Mushrooms
  5. Olives
  6. Italian sausage
  7. Extra cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough: Dissolve sugar in warm water and add yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy. Mix flour and salt in a bowl, add yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead into a smooth dough, cover and let it rise for an hour.
  2. Preheat the oven: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll out the dough: Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  4. Add sauce and toppings: Spread pizza sauce on the dough, add cheese, and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake the pizza: Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Serve: Slice and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
